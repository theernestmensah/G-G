Somalia

About Somalia

Somalia, officially the Federal Republic of Somalia, is a country in the Horn of Africa. The country has a long coastline on the Indian Ocean and the Gulf of Aden. Despite its recent history of conflict, Somalia is a country of great natural beauty, with pristine beaches, stunning waterfalls, and vast mountain ranges. The capital, Mogadishu, is home to a vibrant market and a growing number of restaurants and hotels.
