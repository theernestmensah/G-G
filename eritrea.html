Eritrea

About Eritrea

Eritrea is a northeast African country on the Red Sea coast. It shares borders with Ethiopia, Sudan and Djibouti. The capital city, Asmara, is known for its Italian colonial buildings, including St. Joseph's Cathedral, and art deco structures. Italian, Egyptian and Turkish architecture in Massawa reflect the city's colorful history. The Dahlak Archipelago, a chain of islands in the Red Sea, is home to diverse marine life.
